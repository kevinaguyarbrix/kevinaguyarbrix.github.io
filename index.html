Research interests

I am primarily interested in the area of functional analysis that deals with operator algebras on Hilbert space and its connections to topological dynamical systems, in particular that of symbolic dynamical systems.

Recent

  • The paper \(C^*\)-algebras, groupoid and covers of shift spaces (with T.M. Carlsen) is accepted for publication in the Transactions of the American Mathematical Society.
  • Work in progress: Sturmian shift spaces and their \(C^*\)-algebras.
  • Work in progress: Conjugacy of graphs, with Becky Armstrong, Toke Meier Carlsen and Søren Eilers.

Kevin Aguyar Brix

I am postdoctoral researcher in mathematics based at the University of Wollongong, Australia. I am currently funded by a Carlsberg Foundation Internationalisation Fellowship.

In September 2019, I obtained my PhD from the University of Copenhagen as a member of the Centre for Symmetry and Deformation under the supervision of Professor Søren Eilers.
