Research interests

I am primarily interested in studying topological dynamical systems, in particular symbolic dynamics, and how these systems are encoded into operators on Hilbert space or, more specifically, into C*-algebras.

Recent

  • Work in progress: Sturmian shift spaces and their C*-algebras.
  • Work in progress: On conjugacy of graphs, with Becky Armstrong, Toke Meier Carlsen and Søren Eilers.
  • Work in progress: On ideal structure of groupoid C*-algebras with Toke Meier Carlsen and Aidan Sims.

Kevin Aguyar Brix

I am postdoctoral researcher in mathematics based at the University of Glasgow (Scotland) with Xin Li as my host and funded by a Carlsberg Foundation Internationalisation Fellowship.

I spent a year as a postdoc (also funded by the Carlsberg Foundation) at the University of Wollongong (Australia) with Aidan Sims as my host after obtaining my PhD in 2019 from the University of Copenhagen as a member of the Centre for Symmetry and Deformation under the supervision of Søren Eilers.
