Research interests

I am interested in the area of pure mathematics which concerns topological dynamical systems and operator algebras. At the moment I study symbolic dynamical systems, how they are encoded into C*-algebras, and how much dynamical stucture is remembered or lost in this encoding. Using operator algebraic tools this allows us to derive interesting properties of the original systems.
