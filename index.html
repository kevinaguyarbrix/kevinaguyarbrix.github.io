Research interests

I am interested in the area of pure mathematics which concerns the intersection and interconnection between topological dynamical systems and operator algebras. I study symbolic dynamical systems, how they are encoded into C*-algebras, and how much dynamical stucture is remembered or lost in this encoding. Using operator algebraic tools, this allows us to derive interesting properties of the original systems.

Kevin Aguyar Brix

I am a postdoctoral researcher in mathematics based at the University of Glasgow (Scotland) with Xin Li as my host. I am funded by an International postdoctoral grant from the Independent Research Fund Denmark.

Previously, I was a postdoc at the University of Wollongong (Australia) with Aidan Sims as my host. This was funded by a Carlsberg Foundation Internationalisation Fellowship (Feb 2020 - Feb 2022). I obtained my PhD in 2019 from the University of Copenhagen as a member of the Centre for Symmetry and Deformation under the supervision of Søren Eilers.
